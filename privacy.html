Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

BAVN.io is built privacy-first. We collect only what's needed to make the product work, we never sell your data, and you stay in full control of what the AI can and cannot see.

1. Who we are

BAVN.io ("BAVN", "we", "us") is an AI-powered Chrome extension that helps users fill online forms automatically using a personal profile and cloud memory. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension and website.

By using BAVN, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information — your name and email address obtained via Google OAuth when you sign in.
  • Profile data — information you voluntarily enter into your BAVN profile (college, degree, graduation year, resume/bio, and optionally phone, email, LinkedIn).
  • Answer memory — form questions and your generated or manually saved answers, stored to power the memory feature.
  • Form URLs — the URL of pages where you use BAVN, stored alongside answers to provide context.

We do not collect browsing history, keystrokes, passwords, or any data from pages where you are not actively using BAVN.

3. How we use your data

  • To generate personalized answers to form questions using your profile
  • To save and retrieve your answer memory across devices
  • To authenticate you securely via Google OAuth
  • To improve the accuracy of memory matching for future forms

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the BAVN service.

4. AI and third-party services

When you generate answers, your form questions and selected profile fields are sent to Groq (our AI provider) to generate responses. You control exactly which profile fields are sent using the privacy toggles in the Profile tab.

  • Phone number, email, and LinkedIn are off by default and never sent to Groq unless you explicitly enable them
  • Groq's API is used solely to generate answers — they do not train on your data via API calls
  • Your data is stored in Supabase, a managed database platform with enterprise-grade security
  • Authentication is handled by Google OAuth — we never see or store your Google password

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase with the following protections:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) — enforced at the database level. Your data is physically inaccessible to other users, even with the database URL.
  • JWT authentication — every API request is verified with a signed token. Unauthenticated requests are rejected.
  • HTTPS only — all data in transit is encrypted using TLS.

6. Your rights and controls

You are in full control of your data at all times:

  • Delete any saved answer from the Memory tab at any time
  • Edit or clear your profile data from the Profile tab
  • Toggle AI visibility per field — control exactly what Groq can see
  • Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, all your profile data, answer memory, and account information will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Children's privacy

BAVN is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of BAVN after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@bavn.io.
