How it works
From open to filled
in four steps.
🧩

Install & sign in

Add BAVN to Chrome and sign in with Google. Your account and memory sync across every device you own — instantly.

👤

Set your profile once

Fill in your details — college, degree, skills, resume. Use privacy toggles to choose exactly what the AI is allowed to see.

✦

Generate answers

Open any form, click Generate in the sidebar. BAVN reads every question and creates personalized, first-person answers instantly.

⚡

Fill & remember

One click fills the entire form. Every answer is saved to your cloud memory — reused automatically on future forms. It gets smarter every time.

Privacy
You control what
the AI sees.
Your data stays yours.

BAVN is built privacy-first from the ground up. Row Level Security means your data is physically inaccessible to anyone else — even with direct database access.

  • ✓Row Level Security at DB level — only you can read your data
  • ✓Phone, email, LinkedIn never sent to AI unless you enable them
  • ✓Google OAuth — we never see or store your password
  • ✓Delete any saved answer at any time from the Memory tab
  • ✓No ads. No selling your data. No tracking. Ever.
